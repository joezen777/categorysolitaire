# Starting prompt

Create a solitaire style mobile responsive web app using React/Vite/MUI components.  Instead of a classic playing cards deck we have a card deck that has several different categories.  Make the code modular to easily substitute in new decks but this is an initial configuration.  At the top is the source deck of cards that deals out one card at a time into a draft pile of cards where the user can pull a card from the top of the draft pile. Cards in the source deck are face down so we can only see the back of the playing cards' generic design.  The draft pile of cards is face up.  When all cards are used up in the source deck of cards an empty card slot is shown.  If the user clicks on the empty card slot then the cards in the draft pile are flipped over, the order is inverted so that card on the top of the draft pile is last and the card that was on the bottom of the draft pile is first.  the draft pile shows an empty spot again and the cards are available to draw one at a time again on the source pile.  Below the source and draft piles are five empty card holder slots.  And then below the five empty card holder slots is the play area.  The play area has five columns of cards when the game is started.  The first column should have four cards, the second column five cards, the third column six cards, the fourth column seven cards and the fifth column eight cards.  All cards are face down except for the bottom card of each column.  The bottom card will always be face up.  Only the bottom card can be moved to another column or to one of the five empty card holder slots.  The card deck consists of category title cards that lists the name of the category and how many cards are in that category.  The card deck also consists of category item cards, each category item card will have the text of the item that belongs to the category.  If a category title card is at the bottom of one of the columns in the play area, it can only be placed into either an empty column in the play area, on top of a category item card of its same category, or it can be placed into one of the empty five card holder slots; any other move is considered illegal and the card will make an error sound and drag back to where it started before the player tried to drag it to a new position.  Category item cards can only be placed on top of other category item cards of the same category in the play area or on an empty column in the play area; in the five empty cardholders area the player can only drag a category item card to this area if the same category's category title card exists in the chosen slot, or a category item card of the category item cards same category; all other moves are illegal and will generate a warning noise and the card will drag its self back to where it started before the player tried to move it. If a player places a category title card on top of another face up category item card or the player places a category item card on top of another face up category item card, then all cards remain face up; the cards will overlap so that a smaller version of the category item text is visible at the top of the visible card while the bottom card shows the full face card.  When the player puts category item cards into one of the valid five areas above the play area, a counter will show above the category item card displaying the category title text as well as how many cards have been collected in a "x/y" number format.  When all category item cards for its category have been placed in its category title card deck, the deck will glitter and then disappear in a success noise and the user will receive one hundred points.  The score should show at the top left of the screen.  There should also be a basic menu cog option that allows the user to either restart the game, quit, or turn off sound.
